  • Episode 1

    Pingu's Disadvantage

    PINGU is put out when he loses several games of hide and seek and to make matters his friends are laughing at his efforts to find them. When he sees a ladder PINGU has the perfect solution and climbs up an iceberg from where he is able to see all the hiding places. But the tables are turned when the others remove the ladder and PINGU is left stranded on his vantage point.

  • Episode 2

    Pingu Refuses To Help

    PINGU refuses to help Mother and instead locks himself in his room. Finally, when everything is quiet PINGU leaves room and sees Mother, Father and Pinga hanging out the washing and feels a little left out. As he is realising his selfishness the line breaks under the weight of the washing and only PINGU stepping in prevents the clothes ending up on the ground and he gets the attention of his family once more.

  • Episode 3

    Pingu The Mountaineer

    Having found an old photo in the shed PINGU asks father if they can go up the mountain. However when they set off PINGU soon gets tired. Father encourages and helps him and together they reach the top of the mountain from where they can enjoy the beautiful views from above the village.

  • Episode 4

    Pingu And The Big Fish

    PINGU goes fishing but promises to be back on time. However he forgets all about his promise when he meets his friend. They only catch small fish and it’s so much fun at the waterside that even when it gets dark they aren’t bothered. When his father has to come and find him PINGU is told to come home at once. The fish that PINGU had caught are appreciated by all but he realised that he went too far.

  • Episode 5

    Pingu Shows What He Can

    PINGU and Pinga are playing outside the igloo with their old toys when Pingi comes along and PINGU decides that he is too old for the toys. Instead of playing with Pinga, PINGU takes his bike and starts showing off to Pingi. Pinga meanwhile after feeling a bit left out goes inside to be with mother.

  • Episode 6

    Pingu Clears The Snow

    PINGU helps his father to wipe fresh snow off the car. Deciding this is very hard work PINGU tries to think of a way that would mean that he didn’t have to do it again. He comes up with the idea of an ice garage which Robby helps him make. When Father gets home he is very impressed by the idea of a garage and also by PINGU and Robby for building it.

  • Episode 7

    Pingu Has A Day Off

    PINGU has the day off at school and he wants to enjoy his freedom, not to help Mother with her work. He sledges about and meets all his friends but they all have work to do. PINGU however is still going to enjoy freedom and it is not until he realises that even the mother bird is working looking for food for her young that he remembers his own mother. He rushes home and tells Mother to take a rest.

  • Episode 8

    Pingu The Archer

    PINGU rushes off to his grandfather as he has found a bow and arrow to play with. Grandfather shows PINGU how to shoot the bow and arrow but he doesn’t pay enough attention and shoots at and breaks several things before Grandfather sends him home. When he gets home PINGU is given a job to do as punishment but manages to remain cheerful.

  • Episode 9

    Pingu And The Magnet

    PINGU discovers a magnet and decides to use it to dupe a friend. They play hit the spot, PINGU with a metal ball, his friend with a wooden one. Sure enough PINGU wins every time. However the tables are turned when his friend discovers PINGU's trick and turns it back on him. In the end all cheating is finished when Father unwittingly drives off with the magnet.

  • Episode 10

    Pingu Gets Help

    PINGU is playing with his boat on the water. The boat keeps sinking because of a leaking hull but usually PINGU is just able to rescue it but then it gets just too far out of reach. A passer-by sees what is happening and uses their ski to edge out and rescue the boat; unfortunately in the process he falls in and loses his skiis. Luckily the stranger doesn’t seem too angry and PINGU is delighted to have his boat back.

  • Episode 11

    Pingu In Paradise

    PINGU is playing with his toys when he falls asleep. In his dream he sees trees growing sweets, fish flying into his mouth and huge lollies sprouting like flowers. PINGU is enjoying his dream until Pinga wakes him. All the same she surprises him with something sweet.

  • Episode 12

    Pingu's Dangerous Joke

    PINGU meets his grandfather on his way home from school. PINGU is rather full of himself and wants to play a thoughtless trick on Grandfather and it is not until the last minute that PINGU realises that Grandfather is actually in danger. However he is forgiven and having learnt his lesson is thankful that nothing terrible happened.

  • Episode 13

    Pingu The Pilot

    PINGU and Pinga area playing with their airplane in the igloo and make such a mess they are sent outside. When they see a real plane fly over PINGU and Pinga decide to build their own one and happily play at being pilot and passenger.

  • Episode 14

    Pingu Teases Pinga

    PINGU is constantly annoying and playing tricks on Pinga until she almost starts to cry. Father sees what is going on and stops PINGU by pestering him in the same way as he pesters Pinga. PINGU doesn’t like it but like Pinga, he can’t do anything about it. Having learnt his lesson, PINGU and Pinga play happily together once again.

  • Episode 15

    Pingu's Wish

    PINGU sees a magic kit in the shop are really wants it. He tries to put pressure on everyone, convinced he can’t live without it. At last seeing PINGU wishing so hard for something his parents decide to get it for him. Although it is not Christmas they surprise PINGU with a tree and the present and he is delighted.

  • Episode 16

    Pingu Is Curious

    It is PINGU’s birthday and he discovers a large present on the table. He wants to open it but he is told he has to wait. He is very impatient and he tries in all sorts of ways to secretly find out what his present might be. In doing so he knocks the heavy parcel falls from the table and when he is finally allowed to open the parcel he finds the cake inside it is somewhat squashed. In the end however the cake tastes just as good so everyone is happy again.

  • Episode 17

    Pingu Gets Organaised

    Father demands that PINGU puts everything in its right place. PINGU puts all his toys into his toy chest and then puts Pinga’s toys neatly in their places. Whilst he is putting her toys away Pinga has taken PINGU’s locomotive out of the chest and is playing with it outside. Pingu tries to take it away from her to put it away again but Father encourages them to go out side and play together. When Father comes back there are toys all over the floor again but he realises that children will always make a mess and stops worrying about it.

  • Episode 18

    Pingu Builds A Tower

    PINGU sees a picture of the Tower of Babel in a book and wants to build a tower like it where he can be higher than the birds. However in the end the tower collapses when Pinga starts taking the lower blocks back out. Although PINGU suffers nothing more than a black eye he realises that such a tower is not a reality.

  • Episode 19

    Pingu The King

    Pinga discovers a book about kingdoms and PINGU thinks that it would be great to be king. He plays the king and his friends help to play out the scenes. However when he gets too grandiose and starts ordering his 'inferiors' they decide to leave the one sided game.

  • Episode 20

    Pingu The Baker

    PINGU wants to bake a cake himself after he learns to do so at the bakery. However his efforts are rather clumsy and he accidentally adds extra baking powder. The result turns out to be rather unusual and oddly shaped but the taste is good all the same and the whole family enjoys it.

  • Episode 21

    Pingu Helps Grandfather

    The PINGU family get a call from Grandfather asking for their help. When there seem to be endless delays setting off in the snowmobile PINGU decides to go on foot and arrives at Grandfather's first. By the time Mother and Father arrive PINGU has already seen to Grandfather's foot and made a temporary splint for until they can get Grandfather to the doctor.

  • Episode 22

    Pingu On A Bad Day

    PINGU gets up and stumbles over some of his toys, has a bad journey to school and can't answer the questions the teacher asks him. His day gets worse and worse until he is cheered up by a doing a trick with some ice cream that someone has dropped.

  • Episode 23

    Pingu Loses The Bet

    PINGU is showing off and bets Pinga that he can shoot balls at a can better than she can. At first PINGU wins and gets the teddybear prize however seeing her distress the stallholder gives Pinga some help and arranges for her to win the prize from PINGU. All the same they are happy to share the bear and return home content.

  • Episode 24

    Pingu And His Cup

    PINGU is doing very badly at his sports day. When it comes to the last event, the race PINGU does unexpectedly badly but it then emerges that he stopped to help a friend who had hurt himself meaning that Robby wins the race. He is given the cup for his kind behaviour. When PINGU goes home to show off the cup he places it on the side of the stove where is melts; luckily he has a photograph to prove he got the top prize.
